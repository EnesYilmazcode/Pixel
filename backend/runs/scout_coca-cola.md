# Scout brief — Coca-Cola

## Tactics
- **Product Purity Focus** — Strip away all unnecessary background elements, leaving only our iconic Coca-Cola bottle or can in sharp focus against a simple, vibrant backdrop that amplifies its refreshing allure. Let the product own the frame, boldly.  _(via Pepsi (minimal background clutter keeps fixation on the product and tagline) and Liquid Death (minimal scene so the can dominates).)_
- **Logo as Beacon** — Magnify and center our legendary Coca-Cola script logo, letting its vibrant white pop against an energetic red or joyful scene. Ensure its commanding presence draws the eye immediately, with any other delightful visual elements arranged respectfully around its grandeur, never overshadowing it.  _(via Dr Pepper (oversized centered logo, faces kept to the periphery so they don't steal gaze from the brand mark) and Pepsi (white logo at high contrast).)_
- **Hyper-Saturation Blast** — Amplify the saturation of our Coca-Cola product – think exploding reds and effervescent bubbles – making it glow with an almost unreal, joyful intensity. Contrast this vividness against a slightly muted, yet still warm and inviting, background to ensure our product is the undeniable, hyper-real superstar.  _(via McDonald's (the product hyper-saturated against a muted ground; the food is the focal point) and Liquid Death (the tallboy can centered and high-contrast).)_

## Retrieved rivals
- Pepsi: high-contrast CTA
- Dr Pepper: bold center logo lockup
- McDonald's: appetite-color pop
- Liquid Death: irreverent contrast can
